The Legend

Eight feet tall.
Eight hundred years.

In the deserts of Nevada, a U.S. Air Force observer named Charles Hall claimed to have lived and worked beside a real extraterrestrial race in the 1960s. He called them the Tall Whites — beings over eight feet tall, with chalk-white skin, fine platinum hair, and large wraparound blue eyes. They were said to live nearly eight centuries, move faster than the eye, and learn our tongue in a season.
